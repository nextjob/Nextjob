Pre-Ship

The system supports an alternate form of shipping called pre-ship.  In this mode shipping documents are printed at order entry time.  The shipping documents created with this form of shipping do not have the quantity shipped or date shipped fields filled in.  It is the duty of the shipping clerk to enter this information on the paper work at the time of shipment.  It is then necessary to complete the pre-ship (via order entry) by telling the system what quantity shipped and the date it shipped to the customer.

When the system is setup to run in pre-ship mode the shipping button is removed from the main menu.



Pre-ship and ship buttons are added to the order entry item detail tab.



Pre-ship shipping dialog.

Pre-ship mode is selected by setting NX_SHIP_METHOD to PRESHIP in the NXT.EQUATES item of the NXT.INCLUDES file.  Changing this equate requires re-compilation of the Nextjob application.
