Customer Maintenance



The Customer Maintenance Module is entered by selecting Customer Maintenance from the main menu.



Once Customer Maintenance is selected, the  Customer Selection window will be displayed.  

Field Descriptions:

Customer Number - Drop down list of customer records. User selects from this list to edit an existing customer record.

Ship To Number - Drop down list of ship to records for selected customer.  User selects fro this list to edit an existing customer ship to record.

Buttons:

Customer Frame:

Edit - User selects this button to edit choosen customer record.

New - User selects to create a new customer record. 

Ship To Frame:

Edit - User selects this button to edit choosen ship to record for active customer.

New - User selects this button to create a new ship to record for active customer.



If  the user selects Edit or New buttons from the Customer Frame the Customer Maintenance window is displayed with the Customer fields active.

Field Descriptions:

Customer Nbr - System assigned customer number.

Status - Customer status, select from the drop down list of:
 Active - customer is active with no restrictions.
 Inactive - customer is inactive.
 Hold Bookings - do not allow new orders to be taken, shipping of current order allowed.
 Hold Shipments - do not allow shipments of currently booked shipments.

Name - Enter customer bill to name.

Addr1 - Enter customer bill to street address 1

Addr2 - Enter customer bill to street address 2

City - Enter customer bill to city.

St - Select customer bill to state from drop down list.

Zip - Enter customer bill to zip code.

Co - Enter customer bill to country.

AR Limit - Enter customer accounts receivable limit.  Once customer's AR balance exceeds this amount shipments are no longer allowed.

Terms - Select payment terms from the drop down list of:
 Net 10
 Net 15
 Net 20
 Net 30
 Net 45
 Net 50
 Net 60
 Net 90 days.

Web Site - Enter customer web site.

Rep - Enter the name of the customer representative.

Customer Contacts Grid:
Contact Name - Buyer or other customer contact name, phone, e-mail address and fax number.
Contact Phone 
Contact E-mail
Contact Fax



If  the user selects Edit or New buttons from the Ship To Frame, the Customer Maintenance window is displayed with the Ship To fields active.

Field Descriptions:

Ship To - User selects Customer Ship To from the drop down list of existing customer ship to addresses.

Name - Enter customer ship to name.

Addr1 - Enter customer ship to street address 1

Addr2 - Enter customer ship to street address 2

City - Enter customer ship to city.

St - Select customer ship to state from drop down list.

Zip - Enter customer ship to zip code.

Co - Enter customer ship to country.

Phone - Enter customer ship to phone number.

Fax - Enter customer ship to fax number

Ship Days - Enter shipment days window (number of days an order can be shipped prior to delivery date).

New Ship to Button -  New ship to information for a customer can be entered by selecting the Ship To New button.
